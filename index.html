Story

Interesting Storytelling of My Names

  • November 2000

    I was born with my birth name

    My birth name is called "mantis shrimp", which is not a cutesy name for a newborn baby. Why my family call me "mantis shrimp"? Time throws back to the time when I was a seven-months-old infant sleeping inside my mum belly. My mum was a foodie during pregnancy. Once, she overate mantis shrimp, and that took me and her lives at risk! Fortunately, we dodged a bullet, and she had a smooth delivery. Thus, I was given the name "mantis shrimp" to record and remember this breathtaking accident.

  • December 2000

    I got my Chinese name from my sister

    When I was born, my sister had very much expectations for me. She hoped that I could study well, so I was named "Oi Lam". Why? Because one of her classmates is called "Chan Oi Lam" and she was good at studying. My sister wanted me to become as smart as her classmate, so she also called me "Oi Lam". What a causal way of naming!

  • September 2006-July 2012

    My First English Nickname

    I sucked at English when I was little. I was asked to have an English nickname when promoting to primary school. The only word popped in my mind was "long”, so I decided to call myself "long". You know what? I didn’t even know how to spell "long", and I randomly spelled a word with four letters. Unsurprisingly, I couldn’t spell the same name every time, and thus my teacher gave me a name which is easy to spell and remember--"Mary". I was called Mary for my entire primary school life. Not bad, right? At least I can spell it correctly.

  • September 2012

    New Name, New Start

    When I grew up, it’s for me to be a secondary school student. I wanted to have a new nickname for my new school life or say I was bored with my old name. I decided to call "Catherine", and I got the idea from my sister. When I was picking up a new name, I thought and thought, racking my brains right and left. I couldn’t find any inspiration. Then I looked at my sister's name--"Katherine". What a pretty name! So I made a little change and named myself "Catherine". Well, you can say that I am lazy or getting inspired by my surroundings, but overall it is a lovely name!

  • September 2018-Present

    My Recent Name

    Are you curious about why I call "Noel" now? Back to the time when I was a Form five student. Once I cleaned up my room, I found a photo of myself taken in grade one. I was holding a piece of paper with the gibberish I spelled--"Noel". I brought the picture to school and shared with my friends how bad my English was. Under the curiosity of us, we checked the dictionary and found it is a real name. It was a coincidence, and I thought there was a miraculous relationship between me and the name. I decided to rename myself as "Noel" when I go to university. That’s why I call "Noel" now.
